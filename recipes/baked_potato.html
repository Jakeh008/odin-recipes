Baked Potato

The Perfect Baked Potato

Cook time -1 hr 30 mins-

Crispy skin, fluffy insides, adorned with creamy butter and a sprinkle of salt. Simplicity in its deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium baking potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons butter

    • Directions

    1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F
    2. Scrub the potato clean
    3. Pierce the skin several times with a knife or fork
    4. Rub the skin with olive oil
    5. Rub the skin with salt
    6. Place the potato in the preheated oven
    7. Bake until slightly soft and golden brown (about 90 minutes)
    8. Slice the potato down the center
    9. Serve with butter on top
